ABOUT TECHNOLOGY

  • by:Camile Campos
  • date:August, 19

Technology advances every day, and is gaining more and more space in our daily lives. Its function is to improve our skills, change our habits, and improve our quality of life.

Bringing us news, new ways of doing things, makes some situations easier. With technology we can find everything very easily, we can study, go shopping, have fun with games, movies, music, and other things.

At home technology brings us comfort, while in companies it helps productivity, and dissemination, causing greater business growth.

As the technology grows and is necessary, new jobs emerge, because to maintain and improve it, many specialized technicians are needed, such as a network programmer, web designer, systems analyst, data scientist and many others. The job market only tends to grow with technology with each passing year.
